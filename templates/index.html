Yearly Count of Crimes Occuring in Toronto

From the illustrated image, it is clear to see an consistent linear growth in the crimes occuring in Toronto from 2014 until 2017. These crimes can consit of theft, robbery, assult and more.

Total Occurances of Popular Crimes Yearly

As seen in the image, aside from Assult, the most popular crimes in Toronto have been consistent in their statistics. Assult however seems to have a consistant growth yearly from 2014 in the number of occurances.

Total Occurances of Popular Crimes Daily

Based on the image you can see that evidently Tuesday's are the most safest days historically from 2012 - 2017 to not be assaulted.

Total Counts of Events at Different Burroughs of Toronto

The burrough mapping is as follows :

Downtown Toronto = 1, North York = 3, Scarborough = 4, Etobicoke / Missisauga = 6

Evidently from this graph it is clear the downtown Toronto has the most offences and North York has the least. This could be due to the density of people in downtown in comparison to the density of people in other locations.

Top 10 Dangerous Neighbourhoods in the GTA

This image is representative of the most dangerous neighbourhoods in the GTA. From a clear initial obeservation you can see that majority of these neighbourhoods are located in downtown Toronto which corresponds to it having the highest count of crimes in the image above.
